Growing Up and Knowing Your Body

Everyone's body is special and unique! This section is like a treasure map to help you learn some basic things about your body and how it grows. It's also important to remember you can always talk to a grown-up you trust if you have any questions.

Special Body Parts

Our bodies have different parts that help us grow, go to the bathroom, and play! Just like you have a special face and favorite toy, there are some parts that are private and only yours.

  • Everyone has private parts that help us go to the bathroom. These parts are important to keep clean and take care of.
  • Some parts of our bodies help us grow and change as we get older. These changes are exciting and perfectly normal!

Healthy Friendships

Friends are awesome! This section talks about how to be a good friend and have healthy friendships.

  • Being a good friend means treating others with kindness and respect, just like you want to be treated!
  • A healthy friendship feels good and safe. You should feel comfortable saying no to things you don't want to do.
  • If you ever feel uncomfortable or unsafe, talk to a grown-up you trust.
